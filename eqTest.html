EQ Assessment

Answer all of the questions below to know your EQ :-

1) I can be very emotionally present with others (friends, family, partners, strangers), but it takes me a while to build trust and share vulnerable things about myself.



2) I often put other people in my life on a big pedestal. (Partner, friends, family)



3) I feel naturally comfortable and safe expressing my feelings and needs to loved ones.



4) I feel very upset when others infringe on my need for space or time alone.



5) I am willing to work through challenges in a relationship before suddenly trying to leave the relationship itself. (If you're not in a relationship, think of how you would handle conflict in a partnership.)



6) I do not become defensive when criticized.



7) I maintain a sense of humor.



8) I try to see things from another's perspective.



9) I recognize how my behavior affects others.



10) I can listen without jumping to judgment.
